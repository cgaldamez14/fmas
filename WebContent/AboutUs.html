Welcome to FMAS


In 2000, FMAS was founded for one big reason….WE LOVE TO TRAVEL!


FMAS currently operates from seven dedicated offices – Los Angeles, Denver, Miami, Houston, New York, Boston and Chicago – allowing us to offer a truly national representation to each of our Subscribers. In addition, we offer our Subscribers valued-added opportunities in local activities, digital and social media contests, tours, motel and hotel bookings.

Our Values


Legacy: Our legacy has been created through shared objectives, the range of subscribers we represent, our national vision, and the enduring impact we generate.


VISION: And we’ve been successfully doing what we do best for over 16 years. FMAS was founded in 2000 by Maligh Gates, an industry veteran who has worked with some of the most influential travel agencies of our time – including: AAA, Expedia, Cheap O Air, Tours for Fun, and Which-Budget. A multi-award winning Agent herself (Gates’s “US Booking Agent of the Year” – 2008, 2011, & 2012), as Founder & Worldwide President, Maligh continues to be the sole Agent for a stellar roster of successful travel stories for almost a million Subscribers nation wide - from Los Angeles to New York to Detroit to Houston to Miami to Portland. Gates works with an experienced team of over 4 highly professional Agents (and 2 uber-dedicated support and administration staff).

TALENT: FMAS’s talent roster – in its fast-growing Flights, Hotels, Tours & Local Activites, Fairs & Festivals – includes the full spectrum in Subscribers’ travel plans. Our hallmark characteristic and proven ability to identify, successfully curate and book Travel plans nationally enables our Agents not only to form that vitally cohesive and strong relationship with each of our Subscribers, but to foster our Agents’ abilities to play an integral role in each of our Subscriber’s travel success.
